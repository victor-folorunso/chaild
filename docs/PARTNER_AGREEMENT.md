# Chaild Partner Publishing Agreement

**Version 1.0**

This Agreement is entered into between Chaild ("Chaild", "we", "us") and the
developer or company registering through portal.chaild.app ("Partner", "you").

By registering a partner account and submitting an application for publication,
you agree to all terms in this Agreement.

---

## 1. What This Agreement Covers

This Agreement governs the relationship between Chaild and Partner regarding:
- Publication of Partner's app under Chaild's developer accounts
- Distribution of the app on the Apple App Store and Google Play
- Revenue sharing and payout terms
- Ownership of code and intellectual property
- Ongoing obligations of both parties

---

## 2. What Chaild Does

Chaild agrees to:

2.1 Review submitted app code and assets for compliance with this Agreement
and applicable store policies.

2.2 Publish approved apps under Chaild's App Store and Google Play developer
accounts using a bundle ID registered to Chaild.

2.3 Handle all App Store and Google Play submissions, updates, and metadata
management.

2.4 Configure the Chaild subscription (RevenueCat entitlements, payment
providers) so users of Partner's app can subscribe and access content.

2.5 Track subscription revenue attributable to Partner's referred users using
the time-weighted usage model described in this Agreement.

2.6 Pay Partner their earned share of revenue on a monthly basis as described
in Section 5, provided payout details are on file and the minimum threshold
is met.

2.7 Notify Partner of any rejection or removal by app stores and, where
possible, provide the reason.

---

## 3. What Partner Does

Partner agrees to:

3.1 Provide accurate information when registering, including app name, bundle
ID, and contact details.

3.2 Submit app code that is functional, original, and compliant with the store
guidelines for its category.

3.3 Ensure the app delivers distinct, substantive functionality that is not
a duplication of another app already in the Chaild ecosystem.

3.4 Integrate the Chaild SDK (`chaild_auth`, `chaild_storage`) correctly and
not attempt to bypass or modify its authentication, subscription, or tracking
behaviour.

3.5 Not use the Chaild SDK to collect, transmit, or exploit user data in any
way not disclosed in the app's privacy policy.

3.6 Keep app content compliant with applicable laws in all territories where
it is distributed. This includes but is not limited to data protection laws,
consumer protection laws, and content regulations.

3.7 Submit updates through the Chaild partner portal. Chaild reviews updates
before submission to the stores. Partner may not publish updates to a Chaild-
managed listing directly.

3.8 Not engage in any activity designed to artificially inflate usage hours,
referral counts, or revenue distributions.

## 4. Intellectual Property

4.1 Partner retains full ownership of their app's source code, design assets,
and original content.

4.2 Partner grants Chaild a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free licence to:
- Build, compile, sign, and distribute the app on Partner's behalf
- Use the app's name, icon, and screenshots in store listings and marketing
  materials related to the Chaild platform

4.3 Chaild does not claim ownership of Partner's code or any intellectual
property created by Partner.

4.4 Chaild owns the Chaild SDK, platform infrastructure, and all related
tooling. Partner receives a licence to use the SDK for the purpose of
integrating with the Chaild platform. This licence is non-transferable.

4.5 If this Agreement ends, Partner retains full rights to their code and
may publish the app independently under their own developer account, subject
to the transition period in Section 7.

---

## 5. Revenue Sharing

5.1 Chaild collects all subscription revenue from users.

5.2 Partner receives 70% of the subscription revenue attributable to their
referred and active users. Chaild retains 30%.

5.3 Attribution is time-weighted. At the end of each month, Partner's share
is calculated as:
- (Partner's weighted usage seconds for a user / total weighted usage seconds
  across all apps for that user) x (user's monthly subscription amount x 0.70)
- Users directly referred by Partner (signed up through Partner's app) have
  their usage hours counted double for that user.

5.4 Payouts are processed on the first business day of each month for any
unpaid balance above $15 USD (or equivalent at prevailing exchange rate).

5.5 Partner must have valid payout details saved in the portal (bank account
or crypto wallet) to receive payouts. Chaild is not liable for delayed payouts
caused by missing or incorrect payout details.

5.6 Chaild will provide a monthly earnings statement in the developer portal
showing the calculation basis for each payout.

5.7 Chaild reserves the right to withhold payment if there is evidence of
fraudulent activity, artificial inflation of usage data, or material breach
of this Agreement, pending investigation.

## 6. App Removal and Suspension

6.1 Chaild may remove a Partner's app from the stores and suspend their
account without notice if the app:
- Violates applicable store policies or laws
- Contains harmful, illegal, or deceptive content
- Causes damage to the Chaild platform or its users
- Is found to be in material breach of this Agreement

6.2 Chaild will attempt to notify Partner of planned removals for non-urgent
reasons and allow a reasonable period to address the issue.

6.3 If a Partner's app is removed by the App Store or Google Play due to
Partner's breach of store guidelines or this Agreement, any unpaid earnings
will be held for 60 days pending investigation before release or forfeiture.

---

## 7. Ending the Agreement

7.1 Either party may end this Agreement at any time with 30 days written
notice sent to the other party's registered email address.

7.2 Upon termination, Chaild will:
- Remove Partner's app from the stores within 30 days of the end date
- Pay any outstanding earned balance above the minimum threshold at the
  next regular payout cycle
- Provide Partner with a copy of their app bundle for independent
  republication if requested

7.3 Partner may republish their app independently after termination. Chaild's
SDK licence ends on termination. Partner must remove or replace the Chaild
SDK before republishing independently.
